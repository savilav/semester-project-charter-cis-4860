    📋 1. Project Title & Description

    🔗

    Project Title: IT Career Launch Campaign

    This project is a focused campaign designed to help me secure an entry-level IT interview by building and showcasing a professional portfolio and essential skills to potential employers.

    🎯 2. Mission · Vision · SMART Goal

    🔗

    Mission

    Demonstrate my capabilities as an emerging IT professional by building real, visible evidence of my skills and presenting it in a clear, professional way.

    Vision

    Within 3–5 years, be working in the IT field in a role where I solve real user problems and contribute to reliable, user-friendly systems.

    Strategic Goal Support

    This campaign directly supports my larger career strategy by helping me transform skills and coursework into tangible evidence of value for employers. It builds a foundation for long-term growth by improving my technical communication, strengthening my professional presence, and creating a scalable platform I can continue to refine as I pursue IT career opportunities.

    SMART Goal (this semester)

    • Outcome: Secure at least 1 interview for an entry-level IT role by the end of the semester.
    • Output: Launch a live portfolio with ≥ 2 projects and ≥ 4 technical skills highlighted.
    • Validation: Collect ≥ 5 peer reviews (at least 1 non-peer) and complete ≥ 10 job applications using the portfolio link.
    • Deadline: Go-live by Dec 15, 2025.

    💼 3. Business Case & Justification

    🔗

    Professional Value

    Demonstrates initiative by turning skills and coursework into real, portfolio-ready projects that directly reflect entry-level IT job requirements. The result is a credible, professional presence that helps recruiters and hiring managers clearly see my potential impact.

    Personal Value

    Provides a long-term career asset I can continue to update as I grow. The process also builds confidence by helping me tell a clear story about my skills, experience, and strengths — both online and in interviews.

    Learning Outcomes

    Gives me hands-on experience with real project delivery — from defining scope and building on feedback to communicating technical work clearly to non-technical audiences. These are foundational skills I’ll use in every stage of my IT career.

    ⚙️ 8. Project Methodology

    🔗

    Assessment

    The IT Career Launch Campaign integrates personal branding, portfolio development, and technical documentation. Because each stage builds upon prior feedback and deliverables—such as resume improvement, skill verification, and career visibility—a Hybrid–Incremental methodology provides the flexibility to adapt while maintaining structure and measurable outcomes.

    Delivery Cadence

    The campaign unfolds through seven iterative phases, each producing a tangible deliverable that contributes to professional readiness:

    • Phase 1: Initiation & Resume Preparation
    • Phase 2: Branding & Professional Presence
    • Phase 3: Project Evidence Development
    • Phase 4: Career Statement & Reflection
    • Phase 5: Certifications & Achievements
    • Phase 6: Validation & Peer Feedback
    • Phase 7: Professional Visibility & Closing

    Each phase concludes with review and adjustment before moving forward. This ensures that every deliverable—whether technical, creative, or professional—receives validation through testing, feedback, or peer review.

    Approaches Considered

    • Predictive (Waterfall): Too rigid for evolving content and feedback cycles.
    • Agile: Flexible but lacked the milestone tracking needed for semester pacing.
    • Hybrid–Incremental (Chosen): Combines structured phases with iterative refinement, ensuring both adaptability and accountability aligned with course deadlines.
